Our Mission

To make self-knowledge radically accessible

For millennia, deep astrological readings required years of study or expensive consultations with skilled astrologers. The knowledge existed, but access was limited. We wanted to change that.

InSight makes it possible for anyone — regardless of their knowledge of astrology — to receive a reading with the depth and precision that was once only available to a few. Your birth data is your key. Our AI is your guide.

We believe self-knowledge is the foundation of a well-lived life. And the stars, if you know how to read them, are one of the most profound mirrors of the self that exists.
